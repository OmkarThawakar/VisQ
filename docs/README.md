## Privacy Summary

- VisQ requests photo-library access so it can index and search your selected media locally on device.
- Based on the current implementation, the app is designed to perform indexing and retrieval without sending your photo library to a VisQ-operated backend for normal app use.
- The app may store locally generated embeddings, descriptions, and related metadata on your device to support search.
